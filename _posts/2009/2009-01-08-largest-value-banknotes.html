--- layout: post title: "Largest Value Banknotes" date: 2009-01-08 14:48:00 source: pblog tags: ["banknote", "billon", "zimbabwe", "highest value", "bank note", "highest denomination", "yugoslavia"] redirect_from: - "/2009/01/largest-value-banknotes.html" --- Hyper-inflation does strange things for the denominations printed on banknotes. There has been some talk of the recent Zimbabwean crisis creating world record denominations on bank notes, and in all fairness 50billion and 100billion are unusually large. The record, however, as far as I am aware, is a 500billion denomination banknote from the dying days of Yugoslavia.
