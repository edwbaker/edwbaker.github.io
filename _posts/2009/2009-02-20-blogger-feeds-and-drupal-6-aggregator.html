--- layout: post title: "Blogger feeds and Drupal 6 Aggregator Issue" date: 2009-02-20 10:22:00 source: pblog tags: ["drupal 6", "same link", "same post", "drupal", "aggregator", "same page"] redirect_from: - "/2009/02/blogger-feeds-and-drupal-6-aggregator.html" --- If you want to import Blogger feeds using the Drupal 6 Aggregator (core module) then you must use the RSS feed (just add "/feeds/posts/default?alt=rss" to the end of your blog's URL). If you don't then the aggregator will list posts correctly, but the links will all be to the same post.
