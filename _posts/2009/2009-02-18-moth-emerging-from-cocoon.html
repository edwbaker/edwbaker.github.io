--- layout: post title: "Moth Emerging From Cocoon" date: 2009-02-18 16:47:00 source: blogger tags: ["cocoon", "moths"] redirect_from: - "/2009/02/moth-emerging-from-cocoon.html" --- A male Saturnia albofasciata moth emerges from its cocoon in this time lapse video. The cocoon was partially cut away to observe development and movement and doesn't seem to bother the moth. After the moth emerges, he expands his wings by pumping his body fluid into them.
