--- layout: post title: "Blattodea Culture Group" date: 2008-04-14 16:55:00 source: blogger tags: ["Borneo", "bbc", "blattodea culture group", "Phil Bragg", "George Beccaloni", "Ed Baker", "Natural History Museum", "Madagascar", "collecting", "Adrian Durkin", "events"] redirect_from: - "/2008/04/blattodea-culture-group.html" --- This weekend saw the return of the Blattodea Culture Group to London, with a well attended meeting at the Natural History Museum.

There were four talks/presentations:

Collecting cockroaches in Madagascar (by George Beccaloni)
A film clip of Adrian Durkin's recent appearance on BBC1
An introduction to the new BCG website (by me)
Collecting cockroaches in Borneo (by Phil Bragg)

There was also a livestock exchange, with a number of interesting species changing hands.
