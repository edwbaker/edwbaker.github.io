--- layout: post title: "English Heritage Tour: Portland Castle" date: 2012-08-21 18:30:00 source: pblog tags: ["english heritage tour", "portland castle", "isle of purbeck"] redirect_from: - "/2012/08/english-heritage-tour-portland-castle.html" --- Part of a tour of locations owned or managed by English Heritage (part 1 of many): Portland Castle - built in the 1540s as one of the Device Forts to protect the natural harbour of Portland from the threat of invasion from the French and Spanish. The Castle has a squat design to minimise target area and curved walls to deflect incoming fire.
