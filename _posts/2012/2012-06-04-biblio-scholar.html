--- layout: post title: "Biblio Scholar" date: 2012-06-04 20:16:00 source: pblog tags: ["drupal 6", "drupal 7", "biblio scholar", "drupal", "biblio"] redirect_from: - "/2012/06/biblio-scholar.html" --- Various Scratchpad sites I work with, most notably the International Commission on Zoological Nomenclature (@ZooNom) and the Natural Sciences Collections Association (@Nat_SCA) both use Scratchpads as a platform the online components of their journals (Bulletin of Zoological Nomenclature; NatSCA News).

In order to provide some increased metadata to aid search engines in indexing this kind of content I wrote the Biblio Scholar module for Drupal which provides some metadata that may be used by Google Scholar (see Inclusion Guidelines) and potentially other services.
