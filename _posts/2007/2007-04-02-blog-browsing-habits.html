--- layout: post title: "Blog Browsing Habits" date: 2007-04-02 22:30:00 source: pblog tags: ["analytics", "promote", "visits", "google", "blog", "posts", "promotion"] redirect_from: - "/2007/04/blog-browsing-habits.html" --- Well I've just checked the statistics of my blogs for the last week or so (using Google Analytics) and noticed some quite interesting, if expected, things.

First of all on days where I posted something the views for that blog went up quite a huge amount, and if I didn't post for a few days it was effectively zero.

I guess this means that I should post more often!
