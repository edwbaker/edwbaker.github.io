--- layout: post title: "Generating a swept sine wave in R" date: 2020-07-22 11:58:00 source: pblog tags: ["sonicscrewdriver"] redirect_from: - "/2020/07/generating-swept-sine-wave-in-r.html" --- This uses functionality from the sonicscrewdriveR package:

install.packages("devtools")
library(devtools)
install_github("edwbaker/sonicscrewdriver")
library(sonicscrewdriver)

Generate a Wave object with a sine sweep from 0 Hz to 1kHz over two seconds:

w <- sweptsine(f0=0,f1=1000, sweep.time=2, output="wave")
