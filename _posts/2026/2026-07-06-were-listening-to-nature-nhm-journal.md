A new feature in the NHM Magazine highlights our work using acoustic sensors and environmental monitoring in the Museum Gardens.

The spread, titled **"We're listening to nature"**, explains how we are tracking wildlife activity and microclimate conditions to better understand how urban biodiversity responds to environmental change.
