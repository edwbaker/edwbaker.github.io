A short piece about my work has appeared in the Natural History Museum children's magazine **Wild World**.

The article, "Adventures in the Museum Gardens", introduces my role as the Acoustic Biology Researcher at the museum and highlights some of the bioacoustics work behind [Nature Overheard](/nature-overheard) and the [Urban Research Station](/urban-research-station).

{% include figure.html img="/imgs/wild-world-adventures-museum-gardens-2026-07-05.png" description="Wild World feature: Adventures in the Museum Gardens" caption="A page from Wild World featuring my work in the Museum Gardens." %}

It is lovely to see this work shared with younger readers, and I hope it encourages more people to listen closely to the sounds of urban wildlife.
