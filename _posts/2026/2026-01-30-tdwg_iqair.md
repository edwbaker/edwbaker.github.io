The TDWG Executive has approved a new Task Group under the auspices of Audiovisual Core to explore extending Audiovisual Core with terms to allow selection of media by items by quality and AI-readiness.

The charter for this new task group can be found at [TDWG IQAir Task Group Charter](https://www.tdwg.org/community/ac/iqair/).

More details to follow.
